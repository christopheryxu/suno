Customer Success Strategy
Deepen Engagement
Improve Coval stickiness and deepen simulation usage:
▸ Create variations in agents to improve voice AI stack
▸ Expand call types, personas, test sets, and templates to capture more edge cases
▸ Build custom metrics, integrate tracing and mirror production using concurrency testing
▸ Expand agent workflows and modalities to include text-based agents, other languages, custom workflows, etc.
▸ Optimize monitors and expand usage across DEV/QA/PROD environments
Grow Team Adoption
Expand usage and buy-in across client Eng/PM/Ops teams:
▸ Continue to translate observability metrics to business KPIs to prove value, focus on high-value workflows
▸ Grow adoption across adjacent teams, source other pain points and use-cases
▸ Integrate Human Review issues into client systems to reduce debugging time
▸ Build behavior and action by setting a weekly cadence to review dashboards and training gaps
▸ Make value visible to budget decision-makers and leadership
Support Retention
Continue Coval platform enablement and client satisfaction:
▸ Prioritize client agent initiatives based on impact and strategic importance
▸ Monitor simulation usage and adoption patterns to monitor account health and reduce potential churn
▸ Setup power user feedback channels to help engineering quickly iterate product and reduce friction
▸ Automate outreach for smaller, low-touch clients to scale customer success operations.
▸ Run QBRs with clients, partner with teams to help build more advanced voice and chat agents
